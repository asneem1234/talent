BOARD COMMITTEES:

1. Executive Committee of the Central Board (ECCB)
   Chairperson: Shri Challa Sreenivasulu Setty (Chairman)
   Members: All Managing Directors, RBI Nominee Director
   Frequency: Weekly (52 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Deals with any matter within the competence of the Central Board subject to general or special directions

2. Audit Committee of the Board (ACB)
   Chairperson: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape, Shri Rajesh Kumar Dubey, Shri Ajay Kumar
   Frequency: At least quarterly (17 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Provides direction and oversees total audit function, reviews financial and Risk Management policies, reviews internal inspection/audit plan, follows up on RBI inspections and statutory auditor reports

3. Risk Management Committee of the Board (RMCB)
   Chairperson: Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Rajesh Kumar Dubey, Shri Ashwini Kumar Tewari (MD), Shri Rana Ashutosh Kumar Singh (MD)
   Frequency: At least quarterly (8 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Oversees policy and strategy for integrated risk management relating to credit risk, market risk and operational risk

4. Stakeholders Relationship Committee cum Customer Service Committee of the Board (SRC cum CSCB)
   Chairperson: Shri Rajesh Kumar Dubey (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Dharmendra Singh Shekhawat, Smt. Swati Gupta, Shri Vinay M. Tonse (MD), Shri Rama Mohan Rao Amara (MD)
   Frequency: Quarterly (4 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Redressal of shareholders'/investors' grievances; ongoing improvements in quality of customer service

5. Special Committee of the Board for Monitoring and Follow-up of Cases of Frauds (SCBMF)
   Chairperson: Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Dharmendra Singh Shekhawat, Smt. Swati Gupta, Shri Vinay M. Tonse (MD), Shri Rana Ashutosh Kumar Singh (MD)
   Frequency: Quarterly (4 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Monitor and review all large value frauds, identify systemic lacunae, monitor CBI/Police investigation, ensure staff accountability

6. IT Strategy Committee of the Board (ITSC)
   Chairperson: Shri Rajesh Kumar Dubey (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape, Shri Rana Ashutosh Kumar Singh (MD), Shri Rama Mohan Rao Amara (MD)
   Frequency: Periodic (7 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Tracking progress of Bank's IT initiatives, approving IT strategy and policy documents, evaluating effectiveness of IT risk management

7. Corporate Social Responsibility Committee of the Board (CSRC)
   Chairperson: Shri Vinay M. Tonse (Senior Managing Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape, Shri Dharmendra Singh Shekhawat, Shri Rama Mohan Rao Amara (MD)
   Frequency: Quarterly (4 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Review activities undertaken by the Bank under Corporate Social Responsibility Policy

8. Nomination & Remuneration Committee of the Board (NRC)
   Chairperson: Shri Rajesh Kumar Dubey (Independent Director)
   Members: Shri Ketan S. Vikamsey, Shri Mrugank M. Paranjape, Shri Dharmendra Singh Shekhawat, Smt. Swati Gupta
   Frequency: At least annually (1 meeting held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Due diligence on 'fit and proper' status of candidates for Shareholder Director; formulates criteria for evaluation of Board, Board Level Committees, and Directors

9. Board Committee to Monitor Recovery (BCMR)
   Chairperson: Shri Challa Sreenivasulu Setty (Chairman)
   Members: All Managing Directors, five Non-Executive Directors including Government Nominee
   Frequency: Periodic (3 meetings held in FY 2024-25)
   Functions: Overseeing recovery of loans and advances

10. Review Committee for Identification of Wilful Defaulters (RCIWD)
    Chairperson: Shri Challa Sreenivasulu Setty (Chairman)
    Members: At least two Non-Executive Directors
    Functions: Review order of Wilful Defaulter Identification Committee

GOVERNANCE PHILOSOPHY:

SBI is committed to best practices in Corporate Governance with objectives to:
- Protect and enhance shareholder value
- Protect interests of all stakeholders (customers, employees, society)
- Ensure transparency and integrity in communication
- Ensure accountability for performance and customer service
- Provide corporate leadership of highest standard

The Bank's governance is guided by:
- State Bank of India Act, 1955
- State Bank of India General Regulations, 1955
- RBI guidelines
- SEBI (Listing Obligations & Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015
- Government of India directives
