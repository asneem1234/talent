===== HDFC SECURITIES LIMITED =====

BOARD OF DIRECTORS:

1. Mr. Neeraj Swaroop
   Position: Independent Director and Chairperson of the Board
   Background: Over 40 years of experience in Financial Services and Consumer Goods industry. Built and led large businesses across geographies in India and Asia.
   Education: Bachelor's degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi; MBA from Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad
   Career: Worked with Unilever, Bank of America, HDFC Bank, and Standard Chartered Bank. Currently a director on various boards and Operating Partner with Kedaara Capital.

2. Mr. Dhiraj Relli
   Position: Managing Director & CEO
   Background: Over 30 years of experience in capital markets, investments, and banking. Leads HDFC Securities' transformation into a diversified financial services powerhouse.
   Previous Roles: Senior Executive Vice President and Head of Branch Banking at HDFC Bank (oversaw 800+ branches); Country Head of Branch Banking at Centurion Bank of Punjab; Key leadership positions at ICICI Bank
   Education: B.Com. (Honours) from Delhi University; Qualified Chartered Accountant from The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India; Advanced Management Program at IIM Bangalore; AI Applications for Growth program at Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Management
   Committee Memberships: Active member of multiple advisory committees across leading stock exchanges

3. Mrs. Vijay Laxmi Joshi*
   Position: Independent Director
   Background: 1980 batch IAS officer of Gujarat cadre. Served in various posts in State and Central Government.
   Key Positions: Joint and Additional Secretary in Commerce Ministry (2011-2014); Secretary, Ministry of Panchayati Raj (from May 1, 2014); Officer on Special Duty in Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation; Head of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India programme); Managing Director of Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation Ltd.

4. Mr. Samir Bhatia
   Position: Independent Director
   Background: Over 35 years of experience in BFSI. Worked in senior leadership positions with reputed companies.
   Education: Chartered Accountant and Cost Accountant
   Career: Worked with Citibank, HDFC Bank, Barclays Bank, and Equifax

5. Mr. Tarun Balram
   Position: Independent Director
   Background: Almost 30 years of experience with HSBC in Corporate and Institutional banking domain.
   Previous Role: Managing Director and Head India - Corporate & Institutional Banking at HSBC, covering Corporate & Financial Sector Clients (local and multinational). Active leadership role in championing HSBC India's Diversity & Inclusion agenda. Mentor to several middle-management executives.
   Education: Commerce graduate from Delhi University; Double Master's in Business Economics and Business Management

6. Mr. Arvind Vohra
   Position: Non-Executive Director (Nominee - HDFC Bank Limited)
   Current Role: Country Head - Retail Assets, Rural Banking and Microfinance businesses at HDFC Bank
   Background: Joined HDFC Bank in 2018 as Country Head of retail branch banking, trade, and forex business. Over two decades of experience in consumer-centric categories across banking, telecommunications, and consumer sectors.
   Previous Experience: Vodafone, Philips, and Standard Chartered Bank
   Education: Engineer; MBA from Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar (1995); Senior leadership program from London Business School (2015)

7. Mr. Ashish Rathi
   Position: Whole Time Director and Chief Operating Officer
   Background: Associated with the company since 2003. Over two decades of experience in capital market operations, risk management, and compliance functions.
   Functions: Heads Risk, Compliance, Operations, Legal, Secretarial and Customer Experience functions
   Education: Chartered Accountant; Graduate from Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics
   Committee Memberships: Governing board member of BSE Brokers Forum (BBF); Member of Association of National Members of India (ANMI); Member of Industry Standards Forum (ISF) for stockbroking formed by SEBI

8. Mr. Bhavesh Zaveri*
   Position: Non-Executive & Non-Independent Director (Nominee - HDFC Bank Limited)
   Current Role: Executive Director of HDFC Bank Limited (effective from April 19, 2023)
   Responsibilities: Responsible for business and operations across the country; creates and delivers operations execution capability across diversified product suite to Corporate, MSME & Retail verticals (assets, liabilities, transaction services including Payments & Cash Management, Trade Finance & Treasury, and ATM Product)
   Background: Over 40 years of experience. Led critical functions of Operations, Cash Management, and Technology at HDFC Bank.
   Other Directorships: Non-Executive & Non-Independent Director (nominee of HDFC Bank Limited) of HDFC Sales Private Limited and HDFC Trustee Company Limited (subsidiaries of HDFC Bank Limited)
